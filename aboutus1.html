The Center for Continuing Education (CCE) at Phoenicia University (PU) is a vibrant department which fulfills the University’s mission in:

    making education accessible to a wider audience
    equipping current employees with up-to-date knowledge and in-demand skills
    supporting organizations in their Research and Development (R&D).
PU provides an outstanding level of education that values innovation in learning and teaching, thus preparing new generations of students to become job-ready graduates and lead in their chosen fields. This outstanding level of education is provided by faculty members and overseen by deans who have been selected for their distinguished academic backgrounds and international expertise.

Admission

PU aims to attract and select students who demonstrate promising academic merit and motivation for learning.
Admission requirements are as follows:

Undergraduate In addition to your application form, you will need to submit:

    One passport-sized photo
    A copy of your identity card or passport
    A sealed and stamped envelope from your school that includes the official transcript of your Year 10 and 11 grades
    Your official certificate for the Lebanese Baccalaureate or its equivalent as recognized by the Lebanese Ministry of Education and Higher Education

Career Center

Welcome to Phoenicia University Career Center
The PU Career Center offers employment services to students and employers that recruit them. PUCC services entail job and internship opportunities, job placement, career orientation, interview preparation guidance and more.
For more information, please write to careercenter@pu.edu.lb


Application Steps If Interested Read Carefully

Step 1 : choose your major

  • Research the majors available and compare them to your interests.
  • Seek guidance from our career counseling team.

    • Step 2: Ready to apply

  • fill the admission application. The application form for admission can be filled online (www.pu.edu.lb) or obtained from PU Admissions Office on campus.
  • submite the application and all required documents.

    • Step 3 : What's next ?

  • Sit for the PU entrance Assessment (PUEA) and interview (required in certain majors ).
  • Collect the decision letter.

    • Step 4 : After being accepted

  • Register as a student.
  • Attend new students orientation.
  • Register your classes.
  • Become a past of PU community.
